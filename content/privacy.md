**SessionClick** is developed by MusicApps.eu (Karl-Ernst Kiel, sole trader, Germany).

This privacy policy applies to the SessionClick app for Android.

---

## What data the app collects

SessionClick does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- Your playlists, setlists, and song entries (title, subtitle, BPM)
- Your app preferences (dark/light mode)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself using the built-in export function (which saves a JSON file to a location you choose).

## Permissions used

| Permission         | Why                                                                                                         |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Foreground service | Keeps the metronome running reliably while the app is in use                                                |
| Notifications      | Shows a persistent notification while the metronome is active (required by Android for foreground services) |
| Vibrate            | Powers the "Feel the Beat" haptic feedback feature                                                          |

The app does **not** request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or storage.

## In-app purchase

SessionClick offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Unlock SessionClick") to remove free-tier limits. All payment processing is handled entirely by **Google Play**. SessionClick does not receive, store, or process any payment information. Google's privacy policy applies to the purchase transaction: [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Third-party libraries

SessionClick uses open-source libraries. A full list of libraries and their licenses is available inside the app under **⋮ → Open Source Licenses**.

## Analytics and tracking

None. The app contains no analytics SDK, no crash reporting service, and no advertising.

## Data deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data in your Android settings.
